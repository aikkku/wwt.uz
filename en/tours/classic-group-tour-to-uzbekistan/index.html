Classic group tour to Uzbekistan

8 days. $1,290

About the tour

Arrival in Tashkent. You will be met by our driver and taken to the hotel. Check-in to the hotel from 14:00. Overnight in the hotel.

After breakfast, the classic group tour to Uzbekistan will start with a guided tour of Tashkent - the capital of Uzbekistan and the largest city in Central Asia. the largest city in Central Asia. This day will include visit to the Old City, where the religious center of Tashkent - Hast-Islamic complex - is located. It is here that the famous Quran of Khalifa Osman (VII century). In Hast-Imam you will visit madrasa Barak-khan Madrassah, Till Sheikh Mosque, Kaffal Shashi Mausoleum and Islamic Institute named after Imam al-Bukhim. Then you will visit one of one of the oldest bazaars of the city - "Chorsu". After sightseeing sights of the Old City you will have a ride on Tashkent subway to the center. Coming out of the subway station "Cosmonautov", you will visit the Museum of Applied Art, and then stroll through Navoi Theater to Amir Timur Square. End of the tour around Tashkent. Transfer to the local airport for your flight to Urgench at 20:25. (1093 km, 1 h 30 min). Arrival at Urgench airport at 21:55. Meeting and transfer to Khiva (35 km, 40 min). Accommodation in a hotel.

Overnight in hotel. Duration of the excursion: 5-6 hours

Today you will visit Ichan Kala Fortress, a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage Site. Visit to Muhammad Aminkhan Madrasah, Muhammad Rakhimkhan Madrasah, Kalta Minor Minaret, Juma Mosque, Tash-Khovli Palace, Pahlavan Mahmud Mausoleum and Islam-Khoja Minaret - the highest minaret in Uzbekistan. Free time. At 19:00 transfer to Urgench airport for flight to Bukhara at 21:00*. Arrival at 22:00. Meeting at Bukhara airport and transfer to the hotel. Overnight in the hotel.

Tea/coffee break

*In case of absence of seats can be replaced by train or minivan. Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours

Full day guided tours of the Old City. Visit to Lyabi-Hauz complex, Magoki-Attori Mosque, Chor-Minor Madrasah, trading domes, Kalyan Minaret, Kalyan Mosque and Miri-Arab Madrasah, Ulugbek Madrasah and Abdulazizkhan Madrasah, Ark Fortress, Bolo-Hauz Mosque, Samanid Mausoleum and Chashma-Ayub Mausoleum. Show at Nadir Diwan Begi Madrasa and dinner at the national house. Overnight at the hotel.

Tea/coffee break

Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours

Drive outside the city, where the summer residence of the last Emir of Bukhara - Sitorai Mokhi-Khosa - is located. The architecture of this magnificent palace intricately combines elements of oriental and Russian decor. Continuation of the excursion in the mausoleum of Bahauddin Naqshbandi - the founder of the Sufi order Naqshbandiya and in the necropolis of Chor-Bakr, where Juibar Sufi sheikhs are buried. In the afternoon departure from Bukhara and transfer to Samarkand (470 km, 4-5 hours). Upon arrival in Samarkand, accommodation at the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.

Tea/coffee break

Duration of the tour: 3-4 hours

Your guide and driver will be waiting for you in the hotel lobby. This day you will visit the famous Registan Square, which was the center of Samarkand for many centuries, as well as the Bibi-Khanum Cathedral Mosque, the construction of which is associated with many folk legends. You will also visit the Siab Bazaar, where you can buy oriental sweets, fresh fruits and national souvenirs. Besides, you will visit the observatory of Ulugbek - the grandson of Amir Timur, the ruler of Samarkand and a great scientist of the Middle Ages, the ensemble of mausoleums of Shahi-Zinda, which is a magnificent ensemble of mausoleums of Samarkand nobility and the mausoleum of Gur-Emir - the tomb of Amir Timur and Timurids. Overnight at the hotel.

Tea/coffee break

Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours

Continuation of excursions around Samarkand. This day you will visit the tomb of St. Daniel. Then you will have a trip to Konigil village, where you can observe the process of making Samarkand paper from silkworms by ancient technology. Farewell lunch in Samarkand. This is the end of the sightseeing part of the classic group tour to Uzbekistan. At 16:30 transfer to the railway station. High-speed train "Afrosiab" leaves for Tashkent at 17:30 (300 km) and arrives at 19:40. Here you will be met and taken to the hotel. Overnight at the hotel.

Tea/coffee break

Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours

Check out from the hotel before noon. Transfer to the airport. End of the tour.
