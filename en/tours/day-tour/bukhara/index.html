About tour

Our journey will begin with a walk through the Laby Hauz Square, the center of the old town. Here you'll see a large pond, surrounded by madrasahs and caravanserais, and experience the atmosphere of medieval Bukhara.

Next we will visit one of the most beautiful madrasas in the square. It was built in the 17th century and is famous for its mosaics and original architecture.

After Lyabi-Hauz we will go to one of the symbols of Bukhara - the Kalyan Minaret. The height of the minaret is 47 meters and it was built in the 12th century. This majestic structure once served as a beacon for caravans crossing the desert.
