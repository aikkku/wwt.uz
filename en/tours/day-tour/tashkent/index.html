About tour

Welcome to the heart of Uzbekistan - amazing and multifaceted Tashkent! This one-day tour will offer you the opportunity to immerse yourself in the capital's the rich history, culture and modern life of the capital city.

We will start our tour from the main square of the city - Independence Square. You will be able to see the majestic Monument of Independence and Humanism Monument, stroll through the spacious alleys and enjoy the view of the magnificent fountains and sculptures.

Transfer to the religious complex of Hazrati Imam, which houses some of the most important Islamic shrines in the country. Here you will visit Tilla Shaikh Mosque, Kaffal Shashi Mausoleum and the library with the world's the world's oldest copy of the Quran, the Ottoman Quran.

The next point of our route will be one of the largest and oldest markets in the city - Chorsu Bazaar. one of the largest and oldest markets in the city - Chorsu Bazaar. Here you will be able to plunge into the colorful atmosphere of the oriental bazaar, buy souvenirs, spices, dried fruits and enjoy the aromas and tastes of Uzbek cuisine.

Not far from Chorsu Bazar there is a famous madrasa Kukeldash is one of the largest and oldest madrassahs in Central Asia. You will learn about its history and its role in education and religious life of the region.

We will take a break for lunch at one of the local restaurants, where you will be offered to you will be offered to taste traditional Uzbek dishes: shish kebab, manty, samsa and refreshing drinks. Enjoy a delicious meal and relax before the second part of the tour.

In the afternoon we will visit the Museum of Applied Art of Uzbekistan. Here you will see unique exhibits of folk creativity: ceramics, fabrics, jewelry, wood carving, and many other things.

Then we will head to the Amir Timur Square, where you will see majestic monument to the great commander and founder of the Timurid Empire. Take a stroll through the cozy park, surrounded by beautiful Art Nouveau and Neoclassical buildings.

We will finish our tour with a walk along the famous Sayilgoh Street, also known as Tashkent Broadway. Here you will find many cafes, souvenir shops and street artists creating a lively and creative atmosphere.

In the evening we'll return to the city center for a farewell dinner. in one of the cozy restaurants. Savor the exquisite dishes Uzbek cuisine, share your impressions of the past day and prepare for the end of the journey.

This one day tour of Tashkent will give you a unique opportunity to to get acquainted with the rich cultural heritage, architectural sights and modern life of the capital of Uzbekistan. We are sure that this day will remain in your memory as a bright and unforgettable adventure!
