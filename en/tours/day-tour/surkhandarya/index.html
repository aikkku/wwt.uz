About tour

Welcome to the breathtaking and picturesque Surkhandarya region, located in the south of Uzbekistan! This one-day tour will offer you a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the rich culture, history and natural beauty of the region.

We will start our journey with a visit to the ancient city of Termez, one of the oldest cities in Central Asia. You will visit the archaeological museum, which displays artifacts from various eras, including the Greek-Baktrian and Kushan periods. different eras, including the Greco-Baktrian and Kushan periods.

We will stop at one of the local teahouses, where you will be offered to traditional Uzbek dishes: pilaf, samsa, lagman and fresh fruit. fresh fruit. Here you can enjoy the hospitality and relax before the second part of the tour.

We will finish our tour with a visit to the ancient fortress of Kuhna Kishlak, built in the Middle Ages for defense against raids. From here you will have a breathtaking view of the surrounding area and you will be able to take magnificent photos to remember.
