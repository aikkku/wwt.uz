About Khiva

Tucked away in the western reaches of Uzbekistan lies Khiva, a city that feels like a living museum of ancient Central Asia. With its perfectly preserved old town, Khiva transports visitors back in time to a bygone era of caravanserais, bustling bazaars, and grand palaces.

Entering the majestic city walls of Itchan Kala, the historic center of Khiva, is like stepping into a fairy tale. The intricate tile work, ornate minarets, and towering city gates create an atmosphere of enchantment. The highlight of Khiva is the iconic Kalta Minor minaret, standing tall and unfinished, adding a touch of mystery to the skyline.

Khiva stands as a testament to the enduring charm of ancient Central Asia. Its perfectly preserved architectural wonders and vibrant cultural traditions make it a destination that captures the imagination and leaves visitors with memories of a truly extraordinary journey. A visit to Khiva is an opportunity to witness history come alive and experience the magic of a living museum.
